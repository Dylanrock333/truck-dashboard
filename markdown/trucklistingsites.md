# Truck Listing Sites

- CommercialTruckTrader.com — very popular, large inventory
- EquipmentTrader.com — covers trucks and heavy equipment
- IronPlanet.com — auction-based, large selection
- TruckerToTrucker.com — classifieds with strong trucking community
- RitchieList.com — equipment and truck listings
- Bruckner's Truck Sales — dealer based out of Texas/Oklahoma, large used inventory    
                                                          
- Rush Truck Centers — headquartered in New Braunfels, TX; one of the biggest dealer networks in the state

## Belly Dump Marketplaces

- MachineryTrader.com — strong for construction equipment and trailers including belly dumps
- CommercialTruckTrader.com — has a trailer section, decent belly dump listings
- TruckPaper.com — good trailer inventory
- IronPlanet.com / Ritchie Bros. — auction format, belly dumps show up regularly especially from fleet disposals
- AuctionTime.com — another auction site with construction equipment and trailers
- EquipmentTrader.com — covers trailers including belly dumps
- Facebook Marketplace — active for belly dumps in Texas, especially around Houston and DFW
